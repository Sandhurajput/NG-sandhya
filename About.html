About School of Programming, Kishanganj

Who We Are

Youth above 16 years of age from underprivileged or low-income communities who can't afford expensive college education.

Our Students

Our students include school and college dropouts, 10th or 12th pass, as well as degree holders such as BSc, BTech who could not get jobs.

Eligibility Criteria

Anyone from low-income families of less than 15,000 INR in urban areas and 10,000 INR in rural areas. Some level of English is good to have.
